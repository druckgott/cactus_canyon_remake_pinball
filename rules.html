🚀 Skill Shot / Hurry-Up at Plunge
  • 🎮 Start the ball → 👀 Display shows the active Skill Shot
  • 🎯 Shoot directly at the indicated target
  • 💰 Successful shot → Skill Shot award collected

⭐ Bonus X – Cactus Lanes
  • 🎯 Guide the ball through the left or right Cactus Lane (back left)
  • 💡 Each lane lights up a lamp
  • 🌵 Both lanes activated → Bonus multiplier +1
  • 🔄 Lanes reset afterward → can be collected again

⭐ Save Polly Peril – Train
  • 🎯 Play the ball on the Center Train Ramp (middle of the playfield, between Gold Mine and Bart Brothers)
  • 🔁 Hit Train Ramp 3x → mode starts
  • 🚂 Mode active → train stops, Polly must be rescued
  • ⏱️ Timer running → act quickly
  • 🎯 Hit the following ramps during mode:
    • 🎯 Train Ramp
    • 🎯 Bank Ramp
    • 🎯 River Adventure Ramp
  • 🚦 Each hit slows down the train → Polly rescued
⭐ Save Polly Peril – River Adventure
  • 🎯 Play the ball on the River Adventure / Left Ramp (left of the Horse Loop)
  • 🔁 Hit River Adventure Ramp 3x → mode starts
  • 🚰 Mode active → Polly must not fall over the waterfall
  • ⏱️ Timer running → act quickly
  • 🎯 Hit the following ramp during mode:
    • 🎯 Whitewater / River Ramp
  • 🚦 Hitting Whitewater / River Ramp → Polly rescued
⭐ Save Polly Peril – Bank Robbery
  • 🎯 Play the ball on the Bank Robbery / Right Ramp (right side of the playfield)
  • 🔁 Hit Bank Robbery Ramp 3x → mode starts
  • 🏦 Mode active → Polly must be rescued from the bank robbers
  • 💡 Shoot the lit targets / ramps → hit the bank robbers
  • ⚡ Flashing shot indicates which robber holds Polly → hit that one
  • ✅ Polly rescued → mode ends
⭐ Ride 'Em Cowboy – Horse Loop
  • 🎯 Play the ball on Horse Loop / Left Orbit (left side, in front of River Adventure / Right Ramp)
  • 🔁 Hit Horse Loop 3x → mode starts
  • 🐎 Mode active → hero demonstrates riding skill
  • 🎯 Score points for hitting the jets over Marksman Right Orbit
  • ⚠️ If started again through Horse Loop → rider falls off → mode ends
  • ✅ Hit Horse Loop again → complete the mode
⭐ Extra Ball
  • 💎 When Extra Ball lights up → shoot Gold Mine Ramp to collect the ball
  • 💡 Ways to activate Extra Ball:
    • 🎯 Complete Save Polly Peril Train / River / Bank
    • 🚀 Skill Shot
    • 💥 Start Showdown Multiball
    • 🍺 Hit Beer Mug Target
⭐ Bounty Award
  • 💰 Collect the Bounty under the Bart Brothers' heads
  • 💡 Ways to activate Bounty Award:
    • 🎯 Lasso Skill Shot
    • 🔫 Defeat Bad Guy in Quick Draw
  • 🏆 Possible Bounty Awards:
    • ➕ Light Extra Ball
    • 🔫 Light Gun Fight
    • ⚡ Light Quick Draw
    • 🔒 Light Lock (or Award Lock if already active)
    • ✖️ +5 Bonus Multiplier
    • ⭐ Increase Rank
    • 💰 Points (250,000 – 500,000 – etc.)
    • 💎 1 Million Bonus
⭐ Ranking Up – Overview
  • 💡 Indicator: Light above the High Noon Deputy Star shows current rank
  • 🏆 Rank can increase through:
    • 💰 Bounty Award
    • 🎯 Lasso Skill Shot
    • 🔫 Winning Gun Fight
  • 📈 Rank Levels:
    • Stranger → Partner → Deputy → Sheriff → Marshall
  • ⚡ Effect: Your rank influences scoring in Quick Draw and High Noon
⭐ Gun Fight – Overview
  • 💡 Lights: Two Gun Fight LEDs under each Gun Target
  • 🏆 Gun Fight can be activated by:
    • Bounty Award
    • Left Outlane when lit
    • Defeating a Bart Brother
  • 🎮 Sequence:
    • Ball lands in the Inlane → Gun Fight starts
    • Bad Guy Target pops up → Ball is ready for your shot
    • Hit quickly, otherwise only a few seconds until next attempt
  • 🏆 Reward: Rank increases on victory
  • Extra lamp outside how to activate? Fix rule information open 01.03.2026
⭐ Quick Draw – Overview
  • 💡 LEDs: 2 per Inlane
  • 🏆 Activation:
    • Hit all left & right targets (color shows progress)
    • Lasso Skill Shot
  • 🎮 Sequence:
    • Return Lane activates Quick Draw → Bad Guy Drop Target appears, ball keeps rolling
    • Hit Bad Guy → Bounty in Saloon activated
    • Points by rank: Stranger 500k → Marshal 2M (counting down to 50k)
    • Hit Bad Guys stay lit
    • Defeat all 4 Bad Guys → Showdown starts
⭐ Wild West Shootout – Topper Mini-Game (only with Topper)
  • 🍺 Hit 10 Beer Glasses → 🎯 Guide ball to Drop Target
  • 💡 Topper lamp blinks → Display shows "Shootout Mode ready"
  • 🟢 Normal ball play pauses briefly → 🎮 Flipper buttons control target
  • 🔴 Light bar moves over enemies → ⏱️ Timing is crucial
  • 👉 Press right flipper when light is under red enemy → ❌ Do nothing when under green enemy
  • ✅ Hit all enemies → Shootout ends → 🏆 Points / Bonus / Star progress awarded
  • 🟢 Normal ball play resumes
⭐ High Noon Overview
Action Outcome Goal
🔫 Defeat all 4 Bart brothers 🏆 Bionic Bart phase unlock ⭐ Progress Star 1
🎯 Win all 4 Quick Draws 🏆 Showdown unlock ⭐ Progress Star 2
⛏️ Complete the Mine ⚡ Multiball triggered ⭐ Progress Star 3
🐂 Complete the Stampede ⚡ Multiball triggered ⭐ Progress Star 4
🎯 Execute 10 Combos 🏆 Star activated ⭐ Progress Star 5
⭐ Star 1: Bionic Bart
  • 🎯 Shoot Saloon door
  • 🚪 Enter Saloon → battle starts
  • 🏹 Hit Big Bart 3 times → brother defeated
  • 🔁 Repeat 4 times for Bubba, Bob, Bronco, and Baron
  • 🏆 Open Saloon on 5th attempt → Bionic Bart fight starts
⭐ Star 2: Showdown Multiball
  • 🎳 Guide ball over an inlane or special ramp → 💡 Left ramps light left Quick Draw lamps / Right ramps light right Quick Draw lamps → Some ramps / Drop Targets also trigger lamps
  • 💡 Quick Draw lamp blinks → 🎯 Hit the corresponding Bad Guy target → ✅ 1/4 Quick Draw complete
  • 📍 Target positions:
    • 1️⃣ Diagonally right of the right gun
    • 2️⃣ Diagonally left of the left gun
    • 3️⃣ To the left of the Bronco Loop
    • 4️⃣ To the right of the Bank Robbery Ramp
    • 5️⃣ Below the Quick Draw light next to the Bank Robbery Ramp
  • 🔁 Repeat for all 4 Quick Draws → ⚡ Showdown Multiball triggers → ⭐ Progress Star 2
⭐ Star 3: Motherlode / Mine Multiball
  • 🎳 Lock 3 balls in the Mine → ⚡ Multiball starts → 💰 Collect jackpots (green)
  • 💡 During Multiball: Hit lit lanes → ✅ Each counts as 1 jackpot → 🎯 Complete 5 jackpots (orange)
  • 🏆 Hit Motherlode → 💥 Super Jackpot → ⭐ Max score / Star 3 complete
  • ❌ Multiball ends when 1 ball remains → ⬅️ Return to normal play → 🔜 Prepare for next Star
⭐ Star 4: Stampede Multiball
  • 🎳 Hit 3 ramps & 2 loops multiple times → ⚡ Stampede Multiball starts → 💰 Score points / Star 4 progress
  • 🏆 Complete all 5 ramp/loop shots → ⭐ Star 4 complete
⭐ Star 5: Combos
  • 🎮 Execute 10 combos in sequence → 🏆 Star activated → ⭐ Complete Star 5
    • ✔️ Linke Rampe → Rechte Rampe
    • ✔️ Linke Rampe → Loop
    • ✔️ Loop → Rechte Rampe
  • ❌ Combo interruptions:
    • ⛔ Normal targets / Drop Targets
    • ⛔ Slingshots / Bumpers (unless ball lands on ramp/loop)
    • ⛔ Missed ramps/loops
    • ⛔ Timeout / multiball interference
