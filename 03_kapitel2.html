Wild West Shootout – Topper Mini-Game
  • Normal targets / Drop Targets
    • Any shot to normal targets or drop targets interrupts the combo counter
  • Slingshots / Bumpers
    • If the ball hits a slingshot or bumper without landing on a ramp or loop, the combo resets
  • Missed Ramps / Loops
    • If the ball misses a ramp or loop and goes to the outlane or drain, it counts as an interruption
  • Timeout
    • Combo time is limited. If no ramp or loop is hit within the time limit, the combo counter expires
  • Multiball / Other Modes (in certain cases)
    • Some players report that Stampede or Showdown may pause or reset the combo counter
  • During normal single-ball play (no multiball), the combo counter is active
Skill Shot / Hurry-Up at Plunge
  • After starting the ball, check the display for the active Skill Shot
  • Immediately aim for the shown Skill Shot → score points
