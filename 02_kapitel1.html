Kapitel 1: Cactus Canyon Spielablauf

Original Version Playfield 1
⭐ High Noon Overview
Action Outcome Goal
🔫 Defeat all 4 Bart brothers 🏆 Bionic Bart phase unlock ⭐ Progress Star 1
🎯 Win all 4 Quick Draws 🏆 Showdown unlock ⭐ Progress Star 2
⛏️ Complete the Mine ⚡ Multiball triggered ⭐ Progress Star 3
🐂 Complete the Stampede ⚡ Multiball triggered ⭐ Progress Star 4
🎯 Execute 10 Combos 🏆 Star activated ⭐ Progress Star 5
⭐ Star 1: Bionic Bart
  • 🎯 Shoot Saloon door
  • 🚪 Enter Saloon → battle starts
  • 🏹 Hit Big Bart 3 times → brother defeated
  • 🔁 Repeat 4 times for Bubba, Bob, Bronco, and Baron
  • 🏆 Open Saloon on 5th attempt → Bionic Bart fight starts
⭐ Star 2: Showdown Multiball
  • 🎳 Guide ball over an inlane or special ramp → 💡 Left ramps light left Quick Draw lamps / Right ramps light right Quick Draw lamps → Some ramps / Drop Targets also trigger lamps
  • 💡 Quick Draw lamp blinks → 🎯 Hit the corresponding Bad Guy target → ✅ 1/4 Quick Draw complete
  • 📍 Target positions:
    • 1️⃣ Diagonally right of the right gun
    • 2️⃣ Diagonally left of the left gun
    • 3️⃣ To the left of the Bronco Loop
    • 4️⃣ To the right of the Bank Robbery Ramp
    • 5️⃣ Below the Quick Draw light next to the Bank Robbery Ramp
  • 🔁 Repeat for all 4 Quick Draws → ⚡ Showdown Multiball triggers → ⭐ Progress Star 2
⭐ Star 3: Motherlode / Mine Multiball
  • 🎳 Lock 3 balls in the Mine → ⚡ Multiball starts → 💰 Collect jackpots (green)
  • 💡 During Multiball: Hit lit lanes → ✅ Each counts as 1 jackpot → 🎯 Complete 5 jackpots (orange)
  • 🏆 Hit Motherlode → 💥 Super Jackpot → ⭐ Max score / Star 3 complete
  • ❌ Multiball ends when 1 ball remains → ⬅️ Return to normal play → 🔜 Prepare for next Star
⭐ Star 4: Stampede Multiball
  • 🎳 Hit 3 ramps & 2 loops multiple times → ⚡ Stampede Multiball starts → 💰 Score points / Star 4 progress
  • 🏆 Complete all 5 ramp/loop shots → ⭐ Star 4 complete
⭐ Star 5: Combos
  • 🎮 Execute 10 combos in sequence → 🏆 Star activated → ⭐ Complete Star 5
    • ✔️ Linke Rampe → Rechte Rampe
    • ✔️ Linke Rampe → Loop
    • ✔️ Loop → Rechte Rampe
  • ❌ Combo interruptions:
    • ⛔ Normal targets / Drop Targets
    • ⛔ Slingshots / Bumpers (unless ball lands on ramp/loop)
    • ⛔ Missed ramps/loops
    • ⛔ Timeout / multiball interference
