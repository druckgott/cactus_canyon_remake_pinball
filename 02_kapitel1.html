High Noon Overview
Action Outcome Goal
Defeat all 4 Bart brothers Bionic Bart phase unlock Progress Star 1
Win all 4 Quick Draws Showdown unlock Progress Star 2
Complete the Mine Multiball triggered Progress Star 3

Star 1: Bionic Bart
  • Action: Focus on Step 2 → Outcome: Know which target to hit → Goal: Prepare for Bionic Bart
  • Action: Shoot Saloon door → Outcome: Saloon opens / Battle starts → Goal: Hit Big Bart
  • Action: Hit Big Bart 3x → Outcome: Brother defeated → Goal: Repeat for Bubba, Bob, Bronco, Baron (4 repetitions)
  • Action: Enter Saloon on 5th attempt → Outcome: Bionic Bart fight starts → Goal: Complete Star 1
Star 2: Showdown Multiball
  • Action: Hit all 4 Quick Draws → Outcome: Showdown triggers → Goal: Progress Star 2
  • Targets: The 4 "Bad Guys"
Star 3: Motherlode / Mine Multiball
  • Action: Lock 3 balls in the Mine → Outcome: Multiball starts → Goal: Collect jackpots
  • During Multiball: Hit lit lanes → Outcome: Each counts as 1 jackpot → Goal: Complete 5 jackpots
  • Action: Hit Motherlode → Outcome: Super Jackpot → Goal: Max score / Star 3 complete
  • Multiball ends when 1 ball remains → Outcome: Return to normal play → Goal: Prepare for next Star
Star 4: Stampede Multiball
  • Action: Hit 3 ramps & 2 loops multiple times → Outcome: Stampede Multiball starts → Goal: Star 4 progress
  • Goal: Complete all 5 ramp/loop shots → Star 4 complete
Star 5: Combos
  • Action: Execute 10 combos in sequence → Outcome: Star activated → Goal: Complete Star 5
  • Combo interruptions:
    • Normal targets / Drop Targets
    • Slingshots / Bumpers (unless ball lands on ramp/loop)
    • Missed ramps/loops
    • Timeout / multiball interference
